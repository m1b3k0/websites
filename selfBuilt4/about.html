    Our Story

    TONDA was born out of a deep love for the art of pottery and a desire to create beautiful, functional pieces that bring joy to everyday life. Founded by a team of passionate artisans, we've been crafting unique ceramic pieces since 2010.

    Our commitment to quality and sustainability drives everything we do. From sourcing local materials to using eco-friendly packaging, we strive to minimize our environmental impact while maximizing the beauty and durability of our products.
