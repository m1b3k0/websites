    Materials & Craftsmanship

    The Process

    01

    The making of these plates follows a meticulous process that honors tradition while embracing modern techniques.

    02

    It begins with design sketches, conceptualized by our in-house team of artists. Each design is refined through multiple iterations before the mold is handcrafted.

    03

    Once the clay is poured and shaped, it's left to dry naturally, avoiding the use of harsh accelerants to maintain the integrity of the material.

    04

    After hand-painting or glazing, the plates are fired in kilns that reach up to 1300°C, ensuring they are not only beautiful but durable enough to withstand daily use.

    A Commitment to Sustainability

    01

    We believe in creating pieces that are kind to the planet. Our materials are ethically sourced, and we minimize waste by using every ounce of clay to produce as little environmental impact as possible.

    02

    Our firing processes are energy-efficient, and we work closely with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability.

    A Legacy of Beauty and Function

    02

    03
